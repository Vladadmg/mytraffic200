A downloadable Upgrade to Version 2.1 for owners of boxed versions of Mytraffic2004 V3.1  is now available here!

MyTraffic 2004 V2.1 enhances Microsoft Flight Simulator 2004’s AI Traffic. MyTraffic adds airliner, general aviation and military traffic, each implemented in the most realistic manner ever experienced in FS2004. Regardless of type of flying you do, MyTraffic 2004 will provide a truly ‘as real as it gets’ environment.

Features:

2.450.000+ weekly AI Flights – MyTraffic 2004 V2.1 includes over 2,450,000 (yes, that’s correct – almost two and a half a million) AI flights between 6,300 airports in all regions of the world. These flights include international, national, regional, commuter, general aviation, cargo, military operations and even touch and go circuits.

Flights have been generated based on a realistic model - All traffic is generated based on real-life traffic patterns. You will see airlines at airports and hubs which are consistent with their real-world operations. MyTraffic 2004 also includes a set of long range flights based on published airline schedules giving you a high level of realism without the boring uniformity of products that restrict themselves solely to published schedules.

6300+ Airports bustling with AI Traffic – MyTraffic 2004 V2.1 flies AI Traffic between more than 5,900 airports in all corners of the world. All aircraft behave realistically during different phases of their flight. Parking positions are also assigned in an accurate manner. For example, airliners will taxi to their specific company terminal and gates, cargo aircraft will park at cargo positions situated near the respective airport cargo facilities. AI Traffic will behave in the same way even at add-on sceneries, provided that they are fully FS2004 compatible.

Touch and Go flights into more than 100 small airfields.

Airliner, General Aviation and Military Traffic - With MyTraffic 2004 V2.1 you get yourself the only AI Traffic generator that treats all three types of aviation traffic with the same priority. You will not have to compromise one for the other!

1855+ Liveries – MyTraffic 2004 V2.1 includes well over 1800 aircraft liveries and this number will keep growing.

113+ Aircraft Models - From Boeing to Yakolevs and Cessnas to Embraers, MyTraffic includes them all. Also included are frame rate optimized models of the Airbus 318, Boeing 757-300, 767-400, Il76, Il96, Tu154, Tu204, and Bombardier RJ), A300, A310, A319, A320, A320, B757, B767, B777 and MD11, just to name a few. Aircraft which have been newly added to 2.1 include A345, A346, A380, C17, Mirage, F15, Casa 212, Saab 340 and Beech 1900.

Simplicity is the key - MyTraffic2004 V2.1 is simple by virtue. We do not overload our add-on with an unnecessary array of features, menus and other distractions. It is our philosophy to keep MyTraffic 2004 as simple to use, yet as powerful as possible!

MyTraffic 2004 Editor is an advanced AI Traffic configuration utility allowing the generation of thousands of flight plans at the click of a button, all based on user adjustable parameters. Capable of recognizing geographic regions and country borders, the editor makes sure that you see the right traffic in the right region.
