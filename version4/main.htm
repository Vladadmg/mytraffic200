Keeping with the tradition of including never-seen-before features, this iteration of MyTraffic introduces the 'AI Time Machine', allowing users to simulate traffic from any year in the past half century.

In addition to this revolutionary new feature, MyTraffic now includes both Naval (previously sold as a separate add-on) and amphibious AI traffic. Furthermore, the new version includes at least 50 new AI optimized aircraft models (including retired aircraft such as the
Concord) and several completely redesigned versions of existing models.

To accommodate for the AI Time Machine, the internal workings of the Editor have been overhauled to integrate more complex algorithms, support for multi-threading and the addition of a more user friendly method to add individual flights. The Editor now includes data about retired aircraft, old liveries and defunct airlines.

Overall, MyTraffic includes 530,000 daily flights (civil, military, amphibious and general aviation traffic), and now the ability to simulate traffic from any year starting with the 1950s: this clearly places it ahead of its competition.

Features:

3.700.000+ weekly AI Flights – MyTraffic 2006 includes over 3,700,000 (yes, that's correct - almost four million) AI flights between 6,500 airports in all regions of the world. These flights include international, national, regional, commuter, general aviation, cargo, amphibian airlines, military and naval operations, even from carriers, and touch and go circuits.

Flights have been generated based on a realistic model - All traffic is generated based on real-life traffic patterns. You will see airlines at airports and hubs which are consistent with their real-world operations. MyTraffic 2006 also includes a set of long range flights based on published airline schedules giving you a high level of realism without the boring uniformity of products that restrict themselves solely to published schedules.

6500+ Airports bustling with AI Traffic – MyTraffic 2006 flies AI Traffic between more than 6,500 airports in all corners of the world. All aircraft behave realistically during different phases of their flight. Parking positions are also assigned in an accurate manner. For example, airliners will taxi to their specific company terminal and gates, cargo aircraft will park at cargo positions situated near the respective airport cargo facilities. AI Traffic will behave in the same way even at add-on sceneries, provided that they are fully FS2004 compatible.

Touch and Go flights into more than 100 small airfields.

Airliner, General Aviation and Military Traffic - With MyTraffic 2006 you get yourself the only AI Traffic generator that treats all three types of aviation traffic with the same priority. You will not have to compromise one for the other! And not only for current schedules, even going back in history to the beginning of the jet age.

2560+ Liveries – MyTraffic 2006 includes well over 2560 aircraft liveries and this number will keep growing.

161+ Aircraft Models - From Boeing to Yakolevs and Cessnas to Embraers, MyTraffic includes them all. All models are created with GMAX optimized for high frame rates even at very busy airports. Aircraft which have been newly added to MT2004 include older aircrafts like Concorde, Tristar, Caravelle, Metropolitan, newest models like Embraer 170 and 190, military models like the Eurofighter, Tornado and all the Naval aircraft models, and much more.

Simplicity is the key - MyTraffic2006 is simple by virtue. We do not overload our add-on with an unnecessary array of features, menus and other distractions. It is our philosophy to keep MyTraffic 2006 as simple to use, yet as powerful as possible!

MyTraffic 2006 Editor is an advanced AI Traffic configuration utility allowing the generation of thousands of flight plans at the click of a button, all based on user adjustable parameters. Capable of recognizing geographic regions, country borders, and the history of airline fleets, the editor makes sure that you see the right traffic in the right region.
